Army Public School

"Discipline • Knowledge • Leadership"

Army Public School (APS) is one of the premier educational institutions in India, known for its commitment to academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development. Established under the aegis of the Army Welfare Education Society (AWES), APS provides quality education to the children of defense personnel and civilians alike. With a strong focus on values, leadership, and innovation, the school nurtures students into responsible citizens and future leaders. Equipped with modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a rich legacy of success, APS stands as a beacon of learning where every child is empowered to reach their full potential.
